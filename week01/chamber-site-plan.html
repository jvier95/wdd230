Guastatoya

Chamber of Commerce

Reason for the name: Guastatoya has been selected as a thriving town in Guatemala. This town is a great attraction for businesses and investment. Many families are moving to this town and many investments are being made.

Purpose and vision

The purpose of this site is to attract potential investors and businesses to find great options for growth and investment for this beautiful town.

Scenarios

  • WDD 230: Web Frontend Development

  • Business Networking (scenario I):

    Maria is a small business owner in Guastatoya. She wants to expand her local network and learn about upcoming business networking events. She's looking for opportunities to connect with other business owners in the area to collaborate and grow her business.

    The Guastatoya Chamber of Commerce is committed to fostering business networking in our town. We regularly host events and activities to connect local businesses.

  • Investor Information (scenario II):

    Carlos is an investor exploring potential opportunities in Guastatoya. He wants to learn about the economic prospects of the town and the incentives available for investors. He's particularly interested in sectors like real estate and manufacturing.

    Guastatoya offers promising investment opportunities, particularly in real estate and manufacturing sectors. The Guastatoya Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to supporting and attracting investors like you. To explore the specific investment opportunities and incentives available, we recommend reaching out to our Investment Services team. They can provide you with personalized guidance, resources, and connections to help you make informed investment decisions. Feel free to contact us via email at @chamber.com or by phone at 987 654 321.

  • Attractions (scenario III):

    Laura and her family are planning a visit to Guastatoya for a weekend getaway. They want to explore local attractions, find comfortable accommodations, and enjoy local cuisine. Laura is searching for information on the town's top tourist spots.

    Guastatoya boasts a variety of exciting tourist attractions that cater to all interests. Some must-visit places include: 'Aqua park Guastatoya' , 'Central Park', 'Cristo Salva hill', and the 'Rocky River'.

Color Schema

  • Black: Used for headings and accents.
  • Yellow: Used for backgrouns highlights and call-to-action elements.
  • Green: Used for background colors and elements that represent growth or success.
  • White: Used for text to ensure readability.

Typography

  • Playfair Display for Titles (Headings):

    Why: We use Playfair Display for titles because it looks elegant and professional. It's like wearing a suit or a formal dress for important headings.

    Effect: This font makes headings stand out and look impressive, which is important for drawing attention to key information on the website.

  • Font for Non-Titles: Roboto, sans-serif (Used for body text):

    Why: Roboto is used for regular text because it's easy to read on screens, like the text you're reading right now. It's like using clear and simple language.

    Effect: This font ensures that all the information on the website is easy to understand and accessible to everyone. It's like speaking clearly to your audience.
